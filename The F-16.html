The F-16


Overview

The F-16 is a single engine supersonic multirole fighter aircraft. That means that it can fill the postion of almost any aircraft, from air-to-air combat, or even bobming from a high altitude. It was approved for production in 1976, since that time around 4,600 have been built, although they are no longer being built for the United States Air Force, they are being built for foreign militaries.

More information on the F-16

The features of the F-16

The F-16 is the most capable aircraft for air-to-air combat, it has proven itself time and time again. It can locate targets in any weather conditions with its highly advanced radar package that it has on board. When it comes to air-to-ground attacks, it can deliver its payload around 500 miles with precision accuracy. The features of the F-16 are still considered to be the best in any aircraft.

The F-16V

The F-16V was unveiled at an Air Show in Singapore in 2012. The F-16V is the newest configuration of the F-16, the V standing for Viper. This version of the F-16 is the most advanced aircraft in production besides the F-22 and the F-35. The F-16V is only in production to be sold to foreign militaries such as Singapore and Indonesia. This aircraft will be the forefront of new aircrafts in production.
